About "Datanova"

A successful sports forecast always deserves a little bragging — and I’ve been obsessed with predicting game outcomes since my teenage years. Back then, people relied on gut feeling, experience, and the occasional wild bias. But I believed something else: if Moneyball could build a champion team, surely a tournament could be statistically simulated — even if it felt too good to be true at the time.

As a researcher, I once toyed with predicting chemical reaction outcomes using experimental data. The idea was sound, but the datasets were small, and the space between disciplines too wide. The tools just weren’t there yet.

Now, almost unbelievably, it feels like the Big Bang of technology has happened in a blink. What used to be fantasy is becoming reality. Chemists are learning to code. Game results are deciphered through oceans of statistics. Automated reports, personalized insights, and lightning-fast business analysis are all powered by the extravagance of CRM data and cloud computation.

Datanova is the cosmic chapter of my journey — a portfolio born from this transformation. It’s where I expand my skills, revisit the unfinished stories of my youth, and explore new intersections between industries. Each project is a star in this constellation — from visual storytelling and statistical modeling to data wrangling and machine learning.

It is a process of learning by doing, building by breaking, and documenting it all along the way.
