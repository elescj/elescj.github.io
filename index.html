About "Datanova"

Successful sports forecasts deserve bragging rights — ask any fan. My obsession with prediction started early in my teenage years, back when most people relied on intuition, experience, and the occasional biased hunch. But I believed otherwise: if Moneyball could assemble a champion team, surely an entire tournament could be simulated — even if that felt too good to be true at the time.

Later, as a researcher, I flirted with the idea of predicting chemical reactions using experimental data. But the datasets were too small, and the gap between disciplines too wide to bridge with the tools I had.

Then, seemingly in a blink, the Big Bang of technology arrived. What once felt like fantasy is now unfolding in real time. Chemists are evolving into coders. Game outcomes are foretold by oceans of statistics. Personalized reports, deep business analyses, and lightning-fast insights are powered by the extravagance of CRM data and modern computation.

Datanova is the cosmic chapter of my journey — this portfolio is a constellation of that transformation: the expansion of my skill set, the revival of old fascinations, and the crossing into new industries. Each project is a star reflecting my growth as a data explorer. From visual storytelling and statistical modeling to data wrangling and machine learning, I’m learning by doing, building by breaking, and documenting it all along the way.
